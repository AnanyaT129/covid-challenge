How to register

If you are a new patient, you're in the right place! Fillng out the form to the right will enable our system to assess your risk level and pass your case on to healthcare professionals.

To register, please fill out the form to the right with the correct information. When you get to the contact tracing section, please try to fill it out as thoroughly as possible to help us alert those who may have been exposed. It is okay if you cannot remember all of the places you have been.

When you get to the end of the form, you will see your automatically evaluated risk level. This does not mean you should not continue to follow the CDC's guidance, maintain proper hygiene, and self-isolate if you suspect you are ill.
