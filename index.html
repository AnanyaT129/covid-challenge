Need help?

This website is for those registering to be tested for Covid-19, and for medical professionals and administrators overseeing the testing process.

If you are asymptomatic, regardless of whether you have been in contact with someone who has tested positive, the CDC's guidelines do not require you to be tested. This means that your case may have lower priority in the triage.

If you are looking for more information about Covid-19, please refer to the CDC and WHO websites.
